What process did you use to track your progress?

For this project the bridge to which we were going to guide was chosen, later a timeline was made to assign our zoom meetings in which we solved each of the tasks, we resolved doubts and we also showed the progress of our bridge. Although they did not go frequently but at least 1 or 2 times there were weekly meetings outside the class to be able to do the homework. the link of our timeline will be in the references to be able to have more detail of how we had our meetings.

  • What tools did you use to help you in your planning? (MS Teams, Google docs, whatsapp, Gantt Chart, joblists, etc...)

    For the tools used, it was a WhatsApp group to be able to have communication between colleagues, we used the Zoom application to be able to share ideas, doubts and that we were all working at the same time but in a pertinent schedule where all the members were available. In addition, the drive tool was used to be able to have a shared document and everyone would contribute and write in the tasks.
