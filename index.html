Introduction

Genios unidos presents the elaboration of the bridge "truss" made entirely of spaghetti where creativity, the application of engineering, and teamwork will be present so that the efficiency of this bridge is great. The motivation will be to be able to arm it, put an effort and our knowledge so that this challenge is the best, in addition to uniting borders

Problem Statement

United Geniuses has the mission of building a "truss" bridge. a common bridge that we see in different places. This team intends to assemble it only with spaghetti, with the aim that 5 engineers from two different schools, and 2 countries come together to carry out the challenge, taking into account the values ​​as a person, with responsibility, honesty, friendship, etc. But mainly this challenge has the mission of demonstrating our knowledge and empathy to be able to work as a team for 6 weeks.
